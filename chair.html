Stereochemistry Rules

Flip Mechanics: A chair flip changes equatorial to axial and vice versa, but "up" stays "up" and "down" stays "down".

Stability: The most stable conformation maximizes substituents in equatorial positions to minimize 1,3-diaxial interactions.
